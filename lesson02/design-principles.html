Fitt's Law

The design principle would have to be Fitt's Law. This is shown on Tiktok's website through the different buttons that are easy to click and easy to navigate through.

Visual Hierarchy

The design principle would have to be Visual Hierarchy. This is shown through Apple's organization of what is important in this case being the Vision Pros.

Hick's Law

The design principle would have to be Hick's Law. This is shown on FC Barcelona's easy navigation. On the top you can see where to buy tickets, merchandise, or pay for memberships.
